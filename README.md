Cyber Scroll takes the addictive nature of vertical scrolling feeds and turns it into a high-octane arcade survival game.

Features:

Infinite Verticality: The higher you go, the harder it gets.

Boss Battles: Every 5 levels, the game shifts to "Boss Mode"â€”gravity turns off, auto-fire turns on, and you must dodge lasers to defeat the enemy ship.

Crypto Power-ups: Collect BITs, BYTES, CORES, and the legendary SOL orb to boost your score and jump height.

Dynamic Themes: Visuals shift as you progress, from Neon City to the Asteroid Belt and Galactic War zones.

Mobile First: Designed to look and feel great on mobile devices (touch/drag controls) while supporting desktop play (arrow keys).
